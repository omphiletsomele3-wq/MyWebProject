About Us

At Hearth & Grain Bakery, we've been crafting artisan bread and pastries for over 15 years. our commitment to traditional baking methods and quality ingredients ensures every bite is exceptional. we start each day before dawn, hand-shaping our sourdough starters and preparing fresh dough. our bakers take pride in every loaf, using time-honored techniques passed down through generations. From our classic baguettes to innovative seasonal pastries, we believe in the simple pleasure of freshly baked goods made with care, patience, and love.
